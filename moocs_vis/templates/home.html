Welcome

Functional Programming Principles in Scala by Professor Martin Odersky was the first introduced MOOC from EPFL. The first version was published on Coursera in 2012 and attracts more than 60,000 registrations from people all over the world. That brings a huge opportunity to explore the data collected from those "early birds" in order to improve the quality of the course, especially the structure of video lectures, as well as derive valuable lessons from it to produce better MOOCs in the future.
For that purpose, we take the large dataset of clickstream events from Coursera, process it to produce a more useful dataset that can be used for data mining, machine learning and visualization. The main focus of this project will be on the visualization of users' clickstream events during video watching time.
You can visit the home page of Functional Programming Principles in Scala on Coursera .
